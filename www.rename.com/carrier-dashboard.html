When it comes to hauling freight, you want access to carrier technology that gives you greater freedom over-the-road.

Whether it’s unlimited load searches, faster payment options or automated check calls, it should be designed with the carrier in mind. With so many load tracking options available in the marketplace today, knowing what is best for your business can bring you more efficiencies with every load you haul.

What is Carrier Dashboard?

Carrier Dashboard is a web portal and mobile app designed to give you speed and convenience every step of the way, even on your busiest days. With Carrier Dashboard, you have unlimited access to our free load board with more than 35,000+ loads a week. You can also review load documents, submit quotes, submit check calls online and more.

Ready to get started? Request a login. 

 

BENEFITS OF CARRIER DASHBOARD

Carrier Dashboard was created with the needs of carriers and dispatchers in mind.
SEARCH FOR LOADS

Keep your truck rolling with more than 35,000+ opportunities a week.

SUBMIT A QUOTE

Once you’ve found your next load, easily submit a quote online with a few clicks of a button.

REDUCE CHECK CALLS

Spend less time talking on the phone by submitting location and status updates via the mobile app or web portal.

BOOK IT NOW

View rates on select lanes and book instantly from our load board with a Carrier Dashboard account.

GET PAID FASTER

Upload load documents and invoices directly through Carrier Dashboard. This means less paperwork for you while receiving your money faster.

EXPERIENCE MORE VISIBILITY

Get pick-up and delivery addresses, directions, additional stop information, plus track loads on a map, all through the web portal or mobile app.

On the Road?

Download the Carrier Dashboard mobile app and manage your loads no matter where the day takes you.

Tracking

Speak to your Logistics Account Executive about utilizing Carrier Dashboard Tracking location services to automate your over-the-road check calls through the Carrier Dashboard app on your smartphone.

Automatically provide hourly updates

With your smartphone providing your location, focus more on driving and less on making status update calls.

Save your battery and data

’s tracking service only pings your location once an hour, requiring very little battery or data.

Only track while you’re over-the-road

Your location will only be tracked while you’re hauling a load and will automatically shut-off after the load has been delivered. You can also opt-out at any time.

Easy to set-up

Your Representative will send you a text with a link. Simply click the link to begin load tracking. Don’t have the app yet? The link will take you directly to the App Store or Google Play. Downloading should only take a minute.
