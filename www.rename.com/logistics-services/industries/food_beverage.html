Rely on the knowledge, experience and professionalism from a company with roots in the produce industry.

Whether it’s fresh, frozen or other sensitive freight, trust the experts at TQL to arrange your logistical needs to preserve its quality from pick up to delivery.

Keep your supply chain moving fast and efficiently because you can’t sell what hasn’t arrived.

DEPENDABLE NATIONWIDE CAPACITY NO MATTER THE SEASON

TQL understands the stakes when it comes to:

Temperature sensitivity

Maintaining accurate temperature control eliminates a fluctuation that could be detrimental to your product’s quality.

Shelf life

Fast and efficient delivery options are critical when maximizing the shelf life of dry, fresh, frozen and refrigerated freight.

Shorter transit windows

Consumer expectations are impacting the delivery demands of grocery retailers, creating the need for more access to expedited shipments.

Peak season volumes

Quality, national capacity no matter the time of year.

Quality you can trust

Your reputation is on the line when it comes to delivering consistent, quality products to your customers.

RELY ON THE LOGISTICS EXPERTS

  • Access to food grade capacity when and where you need it
  • Guideline adherence and training to TQL employees on the Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA)
  • Timely load tracking updates
  • Proactive communication
  • Online freight visibility with TQL TRAX
  • Multi-pick/multi-drop load options
  • 24/7/365 coverage and service
  • Expedited services
