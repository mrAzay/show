Our Technology, Your Competitive Advantage

As the second largest freight brokerage firm in the nation, we understand the need for impactful technology to streamline your everyday tasks and manage complicated processes while providing transparency to all your shipments.

Technology for our customers

TQL TRAX is a web portal and mobile app that’s available for all our customers. With a TQL TRAX login, you can quote, tender and track your truckload, LTL and intermodal shipments from the moment its tendered to delivery, 24/7/365. Customers can also access load documents, payment options and reporting insights any time, day or night.
