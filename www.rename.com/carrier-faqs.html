What is Carrier Dashboard?

Our load board is accessible through our carrier-specific technology, Carrier Dashboard. A complementary web portal and mobile app that gives you the freedom to search and quote available loads, submit invoices, view load documents and reduce check calls from your desktop or mobile device.

How can I get Carrier Dashboard?

Request an account here, then access Carrier Dashboard online or download the app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. An account is not required to download the mobile app and search for loads. Any other feature will prompt a request for a login.

Payment

What documents do I need to send to to process my payment?

  • invoice with load number
  • Signed bill of lading (BOL)
  • Lumper receipts
  • Copy of confirmation
  • Plus, any other paperwork associated with the load

How do I send the documents?

The easiest way to send paperwork is through the “Document Capture” feature located in the Carrier Dashboard app. You can also email CInvoices@ .com or fax your documents to 1.513.688.8782.

Do you have any quick pay options?

Our standard pay terms are 28-days, but sometimes you need it sooner. For that reason, we offer 1-day (at 5% off the gross pay of the load) and 7-day (at 3% off the gross pay of the load) quick pay options. Contact ext. 69355 to learn more.

Why haven’t I received a payment for my load?

For payment questions, please have your PO# available and contact our Payment Resolutions Team at ext. 69355 or email prt@ .com.

Detention/TONU

What are the tips for receiving Detention/Layover pay? 

  • Arrive on time for all scheduled appointments, or within the first come first serve window
  • Call, Text or E-mail :
    • Upon arrival to the shipper/receiver
    • Thirty minutes before detention time starts
    • At the first sign of a shortage, load change, paperwork delay, etc. 
    • Before leaving the location with your paperwork
  • Ask the shipper/receiver to notate your check in and out times on the paperwork
    • If they will not notate, notify before leaving the facility

What do I have to do to receive TONU pay?

  • Be dispatched on a load prior to an unforeseen load cancellation
    • If you don’t have complete load details, you aren’t dispatched
    • Obtain the Driver/Carrier Information Sheet to confirm dispatch has occurred
  • Arrive on time for all scheduled appointments, or within the first come first serve window
  • Notify at the first sign of issues or load changes

Who do I contact if I have additional questions?

  • Please contact Carrier Relations at ext. 57000 or CarrierRelations@ .com.
