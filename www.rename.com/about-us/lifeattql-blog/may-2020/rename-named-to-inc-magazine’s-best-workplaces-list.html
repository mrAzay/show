TQL Named to Inc. Magazine’s Best Workplaces List

We are excited and proud to announce that we’ve been named to Inc. Magazine’s Best Workplaces list for the second year in a row.

The companies on the list are determined through a combination of employee surveys conducted by a third-party company and an evaluation of company policies, practices and benefits. Those that rose to the top scored high on topics including trust, management effectiveness, perks, and confidence in the future.

“It is a high honor for us to be recognized by Inc. Magazine, but it is a higher honor to know that our employees think we deserve it,” said Kerry Byrne, TQL President.

TQL’s culture is built on its Winning Principles of integrity, exceeding expectations, teamwork and forthrightness. These ideals guide the way we treat each other, our customers and our carriers. They also guide the way we support the communities in which we work and live. 

One way TQL has continued its community outreach during the COVID-19 outbreak is through its Moves that Matter program that recently shifted into full gear. Moves that Matter covers the cost of moving donated loads to nonprofits and other charitable organization. Over the last six weeks TQL has helped moved critical medical supplies to COVID-19 hot zones, as well as over 600 thousand of pounds of food and water to food banks feeding families hard hit by this crisis. In 2019, 82% of our employees participated in our TQL Cares program, donating $2 million and more than 6,000 volunteer hours from the company and our employees.

If you’d like to be a part of our award-winning team, apply today.
