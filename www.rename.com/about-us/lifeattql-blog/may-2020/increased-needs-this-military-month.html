Increased Needs This Military Month

During times like these, supporting one another is what it takes to come out stronger on the other side. Here are some ways for us to do that together:

Two Ways to Help our Military During Times Like These

1. SEND A CARE PACKAGE
From May 1-21, digitally build a care package, free-of-charge, and personalize with a note. will print the note, buy the snacks and ship to a soldier served by the USO.

2. MOVE A LOAD & WE DONATE
Book a load with between May 11-17 and we will donate a portion of each load to the DAV and USO through the Hauls for Heroes program, adding to the $224,000 raised since 2009.

Click here for details on Care Packages and our Hauls for Heroes program.

We Deliver More Than Freight

In these unprecedented times, moving charitable loads is more important than ever. is proud to play a part in helping our communities combat current challenges through our Moves that Matters program. Click here to see if your load qualifies and to learn more.

Send a FREE Card to our Brave Healthcare Workers

Show support for healthcare workers on the frontlines of COVID-19 serving and supporting our communities by sending a complimentary card of appreciation. will handle the printing and postage. Click here to get started.
