Named a Best Places to Work in IT

We are excited to announce that our IT Team has been named a 2019 Best Places to Work in IT by Computerworld Magazine. This award showcases IT departments that go above and beyond to provide great benefits and an inspiring work environment.

“Our IT professionals are the best-of-the-best,” says CIO George Rewick. “We’re honored to be included among such cutting edge companies.”

’s IT team is constantly building.

“We aren’t really interested in creating something that works, then simply maintaining it,” says George. “We’re about innovation. We don’t roll out something new without a plan for what comes next.”

The key to our success is investing in our people and giving them the autonomy to innovate using the latest technologies.

Our IT professionals enjoy many benefits and perks, including:

  • Personalized career development plans
  • Flexible work schedules
  • Access to the latest technologies
  • Regular onsite lunch and learns
  • Work from home options
  • Casual Dress Code
  • The ability to relocate to our IT hubs in Tampa and Cincinnati
  • Direct access to end-users and company leadership

“We don’t operate like a company IT department,” says George. “We’re a world-class IT enterprise within ’s walls. Our teams are flexible, reorganizing when needed to ensure the right experts are assembled for each project.”

Because the IT Team operates this way, has been able to use a multi-year, multi-million-dollar investment in technology to make impactful advances to our business. Among these are: the use of artificial intelligence to speed up our billing processes; the creation of cloud-based, hybrid-distributed communication tools to enable logistics teams to communicate in real-time, and combining GPS technologies with our in-house systems to improve load transparency for our customers.

If you’re a fearless innovator ready to take your career to the next level, you should consider joining our award-winning team.
