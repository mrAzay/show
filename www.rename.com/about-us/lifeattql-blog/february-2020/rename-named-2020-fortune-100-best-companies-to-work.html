NAMED 2020 FORTUNE 100 BEST COMPANIES TO WORK FOR®

We’ve done it again.

We learned today that Total Quality Logistics ( ) has been named to the 2020 FORTUNE 100 Best Companies to Work For® list. We landed at #78 on this year’s list, moving up 6 spots from 2019, when we made company history being named to this prestigious list for the first time. Now, we remain among these elite companies for the second straight year.

To determine the Fortune 100 Best Companies to Work For® list, survey responses from more than 650,000 employees in the U.S. were analyzed on how respondents rated their company on credibility, trust, respect, fairness, pride and camaraderie.

 “We take great pride at in developing relationships with our customers and carriers, but it starts with our employees,” said President Kerry Byrne. “We’re incredibly honored by this recognition because it’s further evidence we have created a culture that customers, carriers and employees are all proud to be associated with,” Byrne added.

Since ’s founding in 1997, we’ve consistently focused on exceeding expectations and creating a workplace culture that values and encourages those who work hard and play hard. And, we extend our passion into the communities in which we live and work - in 2019, 82% of our employees participated in our Cares volunteer and donation matching program, donating $2 million and more than 6,000 volunteer hours from the company and our employees.

About

At Total Quality Logistics ( ), we create greater supply chain efficiencies for our customers by combining industry-leading technology and unmatched customer service. Customers and carriers turn to us daily to solve their transportation needs with competitive pricing, continuous communication, and a commitment to do it right every time. We move more than 1.8 million loads across the nation annually through our broad portfolio of logistics services and our network of more than 85,000 carriers. In addition, through Cares and the Foundation, our company and employees make the world a better place by donating thousands of volunteer hours and millions of dollars each year. Headquartered in Cincinnati, Ohio, is the second-largest freight brokerage firm in the nation, founded in 1997, with more than 5,000 employees in 57 offices across the U.S. Connect with on the web, on Facebook or on Twitter @ ogistics.
