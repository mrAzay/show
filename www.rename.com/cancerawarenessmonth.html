Together we fight cancer.

Because cancer affects us all, expanded our 10-year breast cancer campaign, Pinktober, in 2019 and declared October Cancer Awareness Month. Together with our customers, carriers, and employees we raised a record breaking $100,000 for the American Cancer Society (ACS) to help fight all types of cancer.

Since 2010, has partnered with ACS, joining their century long fight against cancer. In 2018, ACS invested over $410 million in cancer research programs. Learn more about the fight against cancer and supporting ACS here.
