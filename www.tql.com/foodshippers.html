TQL HAS OVER 20 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE HAULING YOUR MOST TIME SENSITIVE LOADS

We began our business with a focus in produce and have built a reputation of exceptional service and industry expertise. Our customers benefit from our experience with the entire produce supply chain – including extensive experience with family farms, produce buyers, grocery retailers, and their customers. From field to fork we have you covered.

24/7/365 PREMIUM CUSTOMER SERVICE.
We have set the standard in the industry with our unique business model that centers on a single point of contact. TQL is trusted by the top food manufacturers and grocery retailers in the country when it comes to their most time sensitive loads. Customers benefit from greater efficiencies with streamlined communications rather than relying on siloed departments. We provide peace of mind for your supply chain anxieties.

CARRIER CAPACITY.
Our customers have access to a vast North American network of 85,000+ carriers. Our carefully maintained relationships with many of the most reliable carriers in the country and our thorough carrier evaluation gives us the speed and flexibility needed to move commodities quickly and cost effectively.

SUPPLY CHAIN TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS.
Our technology is your competitive advantage. Streamline your daily responsibilities plus enjoy transparency and flexibility with every shipment with TQL TRAX. Our complimentary technology is designed to improve supply chain efficiencies with tracking, quoting and tendering, 24/7/365 access to shipping documents, invoice management and reporting.

THE LAE ADVANTAGE

A phone call with a live person can be a world of difference for business. As TQL’s business model shows, access to one Logistics Account Executive (LAE) means we can accommodate our customers’ needs with the utmost attention and convenience.

DIGITAL SERVICES

Our self-service technology, TQL TRAX, offers access to digital tracking and business efficiencies. Enjoy the ability to quote, tender, track, and pay for freight, knowing the online portal is still backed by live support when you need us.

INTEGRATED TECH SOLUTIONS

TQL offers custom integrations with Electronic Data Interchanges (EDI) and Application Programming Interfaces (API). We also provide customers with tailored reporting of data compatible with your technology.
