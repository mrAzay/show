Responsible Care

Health, Safety, Security, Environmental and Responsible Care Policy

At TQL, it is our goal to be the freight brokerage firm that does it right. We are committed at all levels of our company to working towards continual improvement in the areas of health, safety, security and environmental performance. In support of this commitment, we will endeavor to:

  • Develop, implement, and continually improve our Responsible Care Management System® to enhance our health, safety, security and environmental performance
     
  • Monitor and evaluate our health, safety, security and environmental performance, and take timely corrective actions, as necessary, in accordance with our Responsible Care Management System®
     
  • Comply with all health, safety, security and environmental laws and regulations that are applicable to us in our capacity as a freight broker and other relevant Responsible Care® requirements to which we subscribe
     
  • Maintain a safe and secure workplace for our employees
     
  • Encourage employees to share accountability for continual improvement in our health, safety, security, and environmental performance through training and consultation
     
  • Foster and maintain an open dialogue with, and encourage feedback from, our employees, customers and other interested parties as determined appropriate

All TQL employees should be aware of and are responsible for complying with this Policy. If you have any questions regarding this Policy, please contact ResponsibleCare@tql.com.

Updated: 4/28/2020
